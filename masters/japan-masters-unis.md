# Research Labs for CS Transition in Japan

## Overview
Targeting top-tier Japanese universities for a Master's in Computer Science with a background in Chemical Engineering (IIT Patna) and professional experience as a Full Stack Developer.

## Recommended Universities and Labs

### 1. The University of Tokyo (UTokyo)
**Department of Creative Informatics**
* **Focus:** Designed specifically for students from diverse backgrounds (Physics, ChemE, Bio) to pivot into IT.
* **Sugiyama-Ishida Lab:** Machine Learning theory and applications. Often applies ML to natural sciences (Chemistry/Materials).
* **Yoshimoto Lab:** Boundary between Computer Science and Chemistry. Focuses on Computational Science and First-principles electronic structure calculations.

### 2. Science Tokyo (formerly Tokyo Tech)
**School of Computing / School of Materials and Chemical Technology**
* **Energy Science and Informatics:** Intersection of Information Science and energy/chemical problem-solving.
* **Matsuoka Lab (GSIC):** High-Performance Computing (HPC). High value on the "physics-first" intuition typical of Chemical Engineering for large-scale simulations.

### 3. Kyoto University
**Graduate School of Informatics**
* **Kikumoto Lab:** Computational Science and numerical simulations. Leverages foundations in transport phenomena and thermodynamics for simulation algorithms.
* **Okabe Lab:** Networking and Systems. Values the rigorous mathematical training of IIT graduates.

### 4. Tohoku University
**Graduate School of Information Sciences (GSIS)**
* **Applied Information Sciences:** Focuses on Bio-Informatics and Complex Systems. Transition path from chemical reaction modeling to complex system modeling.

## Application Strategy

### The "Bridge" Narrative
* Combine **Chemical Engineering background** with **Software Engineering experience** (Backend/Distributed Systems).
* Position yourself as a "Domain Expert" in fields like **Cheminformatics**, **Molecular Dynamics**, or **Material Discovery**.

### Key Sub-fields for Transition
* **Cheminformatics / AI for Drug Discovery:** (ML + Chemistry)
* **Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) on GPUs:** (HPC + Physics)
* **Cyber-Physical Systems:** (Backend/IoT + Industrial Logic)

### Personal Competitive Edge
* **JLPT Proficiency:** Current N5 status demonstrates long-term commitment to the Japanese market.
* **Professional Stack:** Practical experience in Go, Rust, and Java (JRG stack) provides a strong technical baseline for research implementation.
